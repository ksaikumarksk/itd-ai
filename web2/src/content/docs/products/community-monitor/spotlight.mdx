Community Monitor is organized into four main sections, each designed to facilitate a different aspect of community oversight. Together, these modules provide a comprehensive toolkit for effective community management.

## Main Modules

<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="Content Visualization">
    - Visualize data through an intuitive heat map layout
    - Larger circles represent frequently occurring topics and keywords
    - Quickly identify trending boards and areas
    - Enable proactive moderation to address unwanted activity
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Content Integrity">
    - Get an overview of community content quality
    - View metrics on total messages, useful posts, and irrelevant posts
    - Understand engagement quality to maintain high standards
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Moderation">
    - Access a detailed table of flagged content
    - Easily view and address spam posts
    - Monitor top tags and reported content
    - Categorize issues by time and frequency
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Search AI">
    - Utilize robust AI-powered search capabilities
    - Get accurate results even for vague queries
    - Discover relevant content through related term searches
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Empowering Community Administrators

With Community Monitor, administrators can:

1. Efficiently manage community content
2. Foster a safe and positive environment
3. Enhance the overall experience for all community members
4. Take proactive action based on real-time reporting
5. Maintain high levels of engagement through streamlined moderation

The tool's intuitive design and powerful features make it an invaluable resource for community management, enabling admins to focus on strategic community growth and member satisfaction.
